Artificial intelligence has significantly improved medical imaging by enabling faster and more accurate diagnosis. Deep learning models, particularly convolutional neural networks, are widely used to analyze X-rays, CT scans, and MRI images. These models can detect abnormalities such as tumors, fractures, and infections with high precision. AI systems assist radiologists by highlighting suspicious regions, reducing diagnostic errors and improving efficiency. In many cases, AI can identify patterns that are not easily visible to the human eye, leading to earlier detection of diseases such as cancer and pneumonia.
